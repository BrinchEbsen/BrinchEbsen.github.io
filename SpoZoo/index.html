Spo Zoo

Welcome to the world of Spo.

Pluck carrots off the ground and feed them to the Spos to turn them into different skins.
Build fences and grass patches to design your own little zoo.
How many of the elusive golden Spos can you collect?

The game saves automatically when the tab is closed.
Keep in mind: Only Spos on grass tiles will be included in the save data.

Enjoy!
